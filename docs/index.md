# About the book

_First Principles of Math_ is a free text book (PDF) introducing the basic concepts of math, namely

- Integers and rational and irrational numbers
- The elementary operations
- Fractions
- Negative numbers
- Geometry
- Algebra
- Equations
- Functions

### Files
